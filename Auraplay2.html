How It Works: The Child's Journey

AuraPlay transforms skill-building into a daily adventure. Here‚Äôs a look at the journey for a child and their support network, from daily play to internal exploration.

1. Play & Learn: 15 Minutes a Day

Children play a series of 15-minute mini-games designed to be fun and engaging. These games are clinically-informed to target and strengthen specific skills related to internal development.

Targeted Skills Include:

  • Attentiveness
  • Emotional Regulation
  • Distraction Response
  • Motion Response
  • Fidgeting Reduction
  • Impulsivity Control
  • Excessive Movement

2. AI-Driven Adaptive Analysis

As the child plays, our proprietary AI model analyzes their performance against key metrics. This isn't about scoring; it's about understanding. The self-adaptive AI adjusts game difficulty and focus game-by-game, ensuring the child is always appropriately challenged to gain skills in the fastest and easiest way.

3. AuraLab: Internal Exploration

AuraLab is an in-platform feature that helps kids with internal exploration. Guided by "Aura," our friendly lab assistant, children engage in activities that help them understand their own feelings and responses, building self-awareness alongside their other skills. This moves beyond simple mechanics to mindful development.

4. Parent Connect: Clear, Simple Reports

We provide a platform for parents to connect with their child's performance. Instead of complex clinical jargon, we generate reports in easy-to-understand words, showing progress over time. This empowers parents and facilitates better conversations with clinicians.
